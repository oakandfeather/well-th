About Community Well-th

{% comment %}

Community Well-th is an umbrella non-profit organization that receives grants and funding for philanthropic aims that are healing, spiritually based, and community oriented.

{% endcomment %}

Mission

We believe people thrive in connected community. Well-th creates sacred physical and virtual space where the mind, body, and spirit of all people flourish.

{% comment %}

Vision

To return Spirit to healing practices through development of human potential for collaboration, compassion, and interconnected wellbeing. Community Well-th recognizes that holistic health encompases the mind/body/spirit of the individual/community/planet and serves as:

  • A value-based wellness community that gives people the knowledge, online tools, physical space, and local care they need to be radiantly healthy.
  • A culture where relationships between people and health professionals are based on mutual respect, personal empowerment, and sustainability.
